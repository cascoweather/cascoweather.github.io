Gardening in Casco, Maine



All of Casco falls in Plant Hardiness Zone 5a.

The average last frost date for Casco is June 1 - June 10.

Vegetables that you can grow in Zone 5 include: asparagus, beans, beets, broccoli, cabbage, cantaloupe, carrot, cauliflower, collard, corn, cucumber, eggplant, kale, kohlrabi, lettuce, mustard, okra, onion, peas, peppers, potatoes, radish, spinach, squash, tomato, turnip, and watermelon.

In summertime, wild raspberries, blackberries, and blueberries grow in abundance in Casco.
